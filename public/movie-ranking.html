The Best Movies According To Fidelis

These are my top 3 movies of all time:

Pulp Fiction

This is a classic by legendary filmmaker, Quentin Tarantino By genre, it is the best Dark comedy film I have had the privilege of watching Definitely recommend 10/10

The Goodfellas

Martin Scorsese really did a madness on this one. One of the greatest gangster films ever made, it is a great movie to put on during a movie night, and is sure to leave your guests on the edge of their seats

The Departed

Another Martin Scorsese classic, this is a really thrilling watch, and is easily one of the most exciting movies I have ever seen
